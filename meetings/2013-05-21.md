## Homework

Write a program that keeps asking you what you ate and how much (in kg) until you say 'stop'. It then prints out how much you ate, summarized by type.

Example:

What did you eat? pizza
How many kg? 1

What did you eat? fish
How many kg? 3

What did you eat? pizza
How many kg? 2

You ate:
3kg of pizza
3kg of fish
